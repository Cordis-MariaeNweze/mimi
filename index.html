Des Filles Entières

Who We Are

Des Filles Entières is a non governmental organisation dedicated towards the empowerement of the girl child in Nigeria.

We are committed to curtailing gender discrimination in our society and ensuring that the girl child is not precluded from achieving her dreams due to her gender.

Our Girls' Stories

Halima's Story

Halima, a 9 year old girl in the nothern part of Nigeria whose parent had precluded from going to school and married her off to a 50 year old man.

During child birth, Halima suffered a really bad case of Fistula and was poorly taken care of.

Six Months after the birth of her child, Halima's husband passed away and she had to go and live in a displaced persons' camp where she was gang raped.

However, our Organisisation met Halima in one of our routine visits to the camp and she was enrolled in school after months of therapy and she is currently awaitng her admission into the university.
